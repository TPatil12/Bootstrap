ICHALKARANJI

Ichalkaranji is a city in Kolhapur District, Indian state of Maharashtra, governed by a municipal corporation. It is known for its textile manufacturing industry and "Manchester City of Maharashtra".

BAJI PRABHU DESHPANDE

Baji Prabhu Deshpande was a warrior, commander of Chhatrapati Shivaji Maharaj of the Maratha Kingdom.

JOTIBA TEMPLE

Jyotiba Temple is a holy site of Hinduism near Wadi Ratnagiri [1] in Kolhapur district of Maharashtra state in western India.

RANKALA LAKE

Rankala Lake is a freshwater lake located in Kolhapur, Maharashtra, India.

MISAL

The taste of misal ranges from mildly to extremely spicy. And there are a lot of varieties as well. Misal is also a popular street food. The dish is always served hot.
